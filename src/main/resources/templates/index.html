Welcome to History Guessr

This is my project for the Spring Advanced Course 2024 at SoftUni.

It is a game inspired by GeoGuessr and TimeGuessr , and I strongly advise you to check them out! The goal is to guess the location and the year of the given historical picture. You can also compete against other players in the daily challenge. Try it out!

Login if you have an account or Register and play a game!
